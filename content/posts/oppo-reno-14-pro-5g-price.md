Oppo has just launched its new phone, the Reno 14 Pro 5G, in India. This phone is expected to be a big hit in the "premium mid-range" market, which means it has a lot of great features like the most expensive phones, but it costs a little less.

### Screen and Design

The Oppo Reno 14 Pro 5G has a big 6.7-inch screen that is very bright and clear. It has a 120Hz refresh rate, which makes everything look very smooth when you're scrolling. The screen is protected by strong Gorilla Glass 6. The phone comes in two cool colors: Midnight Black and Arctic Blue.

### Camera

The camera is one of the best things about this phone. It has three cameras on the back: a 64-megapixel main camera, a 12-megapixel camera for taking wide pictures, and a 5-megapixel camera for taking close-up pictures. For selfies, it has a 32-megapixel camera on the front.

### Performance and Battery

The Oppo Reno 14 Pro 5G is very fast. It has a Qualcomm Snapdragon 7 Gen 3 processor, 12GB of RAM, and 256GB of storage. The phone has a 4,800mAh battery that charges very quickly. It can go from 0 to 100% in just 30 minutes.

### Key Specifications:

*   **Screen:** 6.7-inch AMOLED, 120Hz refresh rate, very bright
*   **Processor:** Qualcomm Snapdragon 7 Gen 3
*   **RAM/Storage:** 12GB + 256GB
*   **Back Camera:** 64MP + 12MP (wide) + 5MP (close-up)
*   **Front Camera:** 32MP
*   **Battery:** 4,800mAh with 80W fast charging
*   **Operating System:** Android 15
*   **Other Features:** Fingerprint scanner under the screen, Face Unlock, two speakers

### Price and Availability

The Oppo Reno 14 Pro 5G costs ₹39,999 for the 12GB + 256GB model. You can buy it starting August 10, 2025, from Flipkart, Amazon, and Oppo's website.
